Santo Domingo Building Project, Ikoyi

Scroll to read

Rising in the heart of Ikoyi, the Santo Domingo Building is set to become one of the most iconic luxury developments in Lagos. This ambitious project by Chief Olu Okeowo reflects his lifelong commitment to architectural excellence, timeless craftsmanship, and the creation of landmark spaces that redefine urban living in Nigeria.

Designed to stand as a modern-day masterpiece, the Santo Domingo Building draws inspiration from classic European luxury while embracing the clean lines and grand proportions that have become synonymous with Chief Okeowo’s architectural signature. The structure is envisioned as a premium residential development offering privacy, exclusivity, and unmatched elegance in one of Lagos’ most coveted locations.

The project reflects more than real estate ambition—it's a continuation of his legacy of building enduring monuments. Each floor has been planned with exceptional attention to detail: generous spaces, refined interior palettes, advanced structural engineering, and panoramic views of Ikoyi’s serene landscape.

Craftsmanship Meets Vision

True to form, Chief Okeowo continues to prioritize structural integrity, artisanal finishing, and materials sourced from some of the best manufacturers globally. The Santo Domingo Building will feature premium stonework, high-performance glazing, and meticulously crafted interior details — resulting in a residence that feels both stately and intimate.

A Landmark for Generations

Beyond its aesthetics, the development is engineered to stand the test of time. Every aspect — from its foundation to its façade — has been designed to express durability, stability, and permanence. It is envisioned as a building that will remain relevant and admired for decades, much like other architectural contributions Chief Okeowo has made across the country.

Redefining Luxury Living in Ikoyi

As construction progresses steadily, the Santo Domingo Building is already being recognized as a future landmark — a structure that will elevate the skyline and set a new benchmark for modern luxury living in Nigeria. This project is not just a home; it is an experience, a statement, and a legacy shaped by a man whose vision continues to influence the landscape of Nigerian architecture.
