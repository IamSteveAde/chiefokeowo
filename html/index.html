A Legacy of Leadership

Meet Sir Prince Dr. Clement Oluwatumininu Okeowo

Sir Prince Dr. Clement Oluwatumininu Okeowo is one of Africa’s most admired entrepreneurs, celebrated for his pioneering influence in real estate development, philanthropy, religious advancement, and national empowerment. A royal prince of Ijebu Ode and Chairman of the Gibraltar Group, he has—together with his wife, Lady (Barr.) Adejoke Okeowo—built one of the continent’s most respected conglomerates. His life embodies excellence, compassion, and transformative impact, uplifting countless lives through generous humanitarianism and visionary leadership.

Featured Projects

A glimpse into the transformative projects led by Sir Prince Dr. Clement Oluwatumininu Okeowo, spanning real estate, philanthropy, and cultural development.

Operation Light-Up Lagos

Revolutionizing infrastructure and public safety with innovative lighting projects across Lagos’ iconic bridges.

Gibraltar Construction Nigeria Ltd

A leading real estate and civil engineering consortium shaping luxury estates and critical infrastructure nationwide.

Sir Olu Okeowo Physiotherapy Centre (LUTH)

West Africa’s largest physiotherapy centre, providing state-of-the-art care, education, and medical research facilities.

Promoting classical and indigenous music, fostering cultural development, and training musicians for global excellence.
