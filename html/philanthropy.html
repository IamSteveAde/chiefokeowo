Philanthropy & Impact of Sir Olu Okeowo

Through his foundation and far-reaching humanitarian efforts, Sir Olu Okeowo continues to touch lives, uplift communities, and bring hope to millions across Nigeria — feeding families, supporting healthcare, strengthening faith institutions, and creating opportunities where they are needed most.

01
Sir Olu Okeowo Foundation

Transforming Lives Through Compassion & Purpose

Through the Sir Olu Okeowo Foundation, he has dedicated his life and resources to uplifting vulnerable communities, supporting healthcare, empowering religious institutions, and feeding millions across Nigeria.

01
Food Bank Initiative

Feeding Families, Restoring Hope

Through the Food Bank powered by the Sir Olu Okeowo Foundation, thousands of households across Nigeria receive steady access to essential food supplies. This initiative is dedicated to combating hunger, supporting low-income families, and ensuring no community is left behind during difficult times.

02
COVID-19 Relief Effort

Caring for Communities in Times of Crisis

During the peak of the COVID-19 pandemic, the Sir Olu Okeowo Foundation launched a large-scale emergency food outreach, providing essential supplies to over 100,000 individuals. This life-saving intervention brought relief to families who faced economic hardship, ensuring they had food, support, and hope in the most challenging global health crisis.

03
Scholarships & Education Support

Empowering the Next Generation Through Education

The Sir Olu Okeowo Foundation has transformed countless lives through its local and international scholarship programmes, offering full academic support to talented students from underserved communities. These opportunities open doors to world-class education, mentorship, and a future filled with possibility — ensuring that no dream is limited by financial barriers.

04
Church Development & Organ Donations

Building Stronger Churches, Enriching Worship

Sir Olu Okeowo is widely recognized for his extraordinary commitment to the growth of the Christian faith. Through monumental investments in church buildings, infrastructure, and world-class pipe organ installations, he has strengthened worship experiences across Nigeria and supported the mission of the Church for generations to come.

05
LUTH Physiotherapy Centre

The Largest Physiotherapy Centre in West Africa

Sir Olu Okeowo made history with the donation of a state-of-the-art Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation Centre to the Lagos University Teaching Hospital (LUTH). This monumental facility—now the largest in West Africa—provides world-class care, advanced equipment, and expanded medical access for thousands of patients annually.

06
Religious Support

Supporting Faith Communities Across Nigeria

Sir Olu Okeowo is a committed supporter of both Islamic and Christian communities, providing continuous donations for worship centres, community development, clergy welfare, and essential religious programmes. His philanthropy strengthens unity, dignity, and spiritual growth across diverse faith groups.

07
Community Upliftment

Empowering Communities Through Care & Opportunity

His commitment to humanity extends deeply into healthcare assistance, educational support, and poverty alleviation programmes. From sponsoring life-saving medical treatments to providing learning resources and supporting vulnerable families, his initiatives continue to restore hope and improve the quality of life for thousands across Nigeria.
