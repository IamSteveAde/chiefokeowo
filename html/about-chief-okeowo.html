About Chief Olu Okeowo

Royal prince of the Funsegbunwa Ruling House, raised in a home built on faith, service, music, and a lifelong passion for uplifting communities.

ABOUT CHIEF OLU OKEOWO

Early Life & Royal Heritage

Royal Roots & Early Foundations

Born into noble Ijebu royal heritage, Chief Olu Okeowo was raised with the principles of discipline, integrity, and service — virtues that would later guide his ascent into one of Africa’s most respected industrial and real estate figures.

Medical Beginnings

His journey began in medicine in the Dominican Republic, later completing his housemanship at St. Nicholas Hospital, Ikoyi. Though he excelled in healthcare, destiny required a broader field — one that would impact industries and cities.

Engineering & National Projects

Driven by intellect and ambition, he pivoted into engineering and infrastructure, contributing to major national projects, including the Illumination of the 3rd Mainland Bridge, tank farms, pipelines, railway wagon conversions, and offshore fabrication.

Rise of Gibraltar Construction Nigeria Ltd

What began modestly in 1992 evolved into a multi-sector powerhouse. Under his leadership, Gibraltar grew into a global force — spanning real estate development, construction, engineering, international property investments, and large-scale housing.

Diversified Global Empire

Beyond Gibraltar, the Okeowo Group expanded into telecommunications and electrification (Bell & Holmes Nigeria Ltd), industrial blending and production (Solomon & Clement Nigeria Ltd), and international sports entertainment via GIBET — a global contender in the gaming sector.

A Legacy That Transcends Wealth

Today, Chief Okeowo stands as a quiet yet powerful force whose influence shapes skylines, communities, and industries. His empire is defined not by wealth alone, but by purpose, impact, and an African legacy built to outlive generations.

Early Life & Royal Heritage

Chief Olu Okeowo descends from the prestigious Funsegbunwa Ruling House of Ijebu-Ode, a noble royal lineage known for leadership, discipline, and cultural depth. Raised in a family deeply rooted in faith, service, and music, his upbringing shaped the values that would define his life’s journey.

From an early age, he demonstrated a profound passion for fighting poverty, uplifting communities, and building value wherever his path led. These early convictions became the foundation for a life dedicated not just to personal success, but to the transformation of people, cities, and industries.

Education & Medical Training

Chief Olu Okeowo’s academic journey spans multiple countries and cultures, reflecting his global mindset and early pursuit of excellence. He began his studies in Austria, where he trained in Medical Technology and developed the discipline and precision that would later shape his career.

Seeking broader exposure, he continued his medical education in the Caribbeans (Dominican Republic), immersing himself in an environment that strengthened both his academic and cultural understanding.

He later earned an Honours Degree in Medicine from the prestigious Universidad Central de Este, a defining milestone that marked his transition from a young scholar to a trained medical professional with an international foundation.

Transition into Real Estate & Business

In 1992, Chief Olu Okeowo took a bold step into the business world by establishing Gibraltar Construction Nigeria Ltd. From modest beginnings, the company has grown into a global, multi-sector conglomerate, reflecting his vision, strategic thinking, and relentless pursuit of excellence.

Gibraltar Construction and its associated enterprises now operate across diverse sectors, including:

  • Real Estate Development: Private estates and luxury homes
  • Civil Engineering & Infrastructure
  • Power Plant Storage Construction
  • Off-shore Component Fabrication
  • Railway Wagon Conversions
  • Bitumen Plants & Tank Farms
  • International Property Investments
  • Telecommunications: Bell & Holmes
  • Sports Betting Technology: GIBET
  • Hospitality: Caribbean developments

These ventures showcase a strategic mindset that combines scale, diversification, and innovation, setting Chief Olu Okeowo apart as a visionary leader shaping industries in Nigeria and beyond.

Music, Church Work & Faith

Chief Olu Okeowo’s life has always been deeply intertwined with faith and music. He serves as Asiwaju Onigbagbo of Irewon Christians and holds the honorary title Knight of John Wesley (KJW).

A major philanthropist within religious communities, he has generously supported pipe organs, church infrastructure, and clergy education. His passion for music led him to found the Gibraltar Orchestra, promoting both classical and indigenous Christian music.

In recognition of his contributions, he has received notable episcopal awards from the Anglican Communion, cementing his legacy as a leader committed to spiritual growth and cultural enrichment.

Philanthropy & Humanitarian Impact

Chief Olu Okeowo is the visionary founder of the Sir Olu Okeowo Foundation, whose impact spans across Nigeria and beyond. During the COVID-19 pandemic, he personally ensured that over 100,000 vulnerable Lagos residents had access to food, collectively feeding more than 2 billion naira worth of individuals in need.

The Foundation continues to operate an ongoing food bank, scholarships, and medical support, reaching both Christian and Islamic communities. Notably, he donated the largest Physiotherapy Centre in West Africa at LUTH.

Chief Okeowo also serves as a long-term sponsor for:

  • Eye care missions
  • Scholarships for clergy and their families
  • Musical training programs, both local and overseas

Personal Life & Languages

Chief Olu Okeowo is a true polyglot, fluent in Spanish, Latin, Portuguese, English, and Yoruba, reflecting his international education and global exposure.

A lover of classical music, culture, and automobiles, he is also known for maintaining one of Nigeria’s most remarkable luxury car collections.

He is married to Lady (Barr.) Adejoke Okeowo, a partnership that underscores his strong family values and commitment to community development.
