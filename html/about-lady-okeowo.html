ABOUT LADY (BARR.) ADEJOKE OKEOWO

Background & Education

Early Life & Family

First child of the late Chief Emmanuel Abiodun and Mrs. Funmilayo Adewumi, Lady Adejoke was raised in a home deeply rooted in faith, service, and integrity — values that shaped her character, career, and philanthropic endeavors.

Academic Achievements

She earned her LL.B from the University of Jos and a Master’s Degree in International Law & Diplomacy at the University of Lagos. Additionally, she is a qualified Chartered Secretary & Administrator, demonstrating her commitment to excellence and governance.

Professional Journey

Lady Adejoke has contributed significantly in both public service and private enterprise, working at the Federal Ministry of Budget & Planning and Assets & Investments Company Ltd. She later founded her own law firm and now serves as Executive Director of the Gibraltar Group of Companies.

Fashion & Entrepreneurship

Passionate about style and culture, she founded Adejoke Garment Industry and created the premium luxury headtie brand, Adejoke Gele, empowering women through fashion and entrepreneurship.

Personal Life & Interests

Lady Adejoke loves traveling, cooking, and reading. She is a devoted mother to Solomon Toluwalashe, Abraham Ibukunoluwa, and Israel Oluwatumininu (Jnr), balancing family, business, and philanthropy with grace and purpose.
